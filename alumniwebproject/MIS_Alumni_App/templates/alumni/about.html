About Us

Welcome to the MIS Alumni Association of Chulalongkorn University. Founded on a rich legacy of academic excellence and community service, our association is dedicated to fostering lifelong connections among the alumni of the Management Information Systems program. As part of Thailand's oldest and one of the most prestigious universities, we pride ourselves on nurturing a network that supports personal and professional growth while upholding the values and traditions of our alma mater.

Our Goals

  • Connect: We aim to strengthen the ties between our alumni by providing a vibrant platform for networking, mentorship, and entrepreneurship. Our events and reunions are designed to reconnect classmates, celebrate our achievements, and inspire the next generation of MIS professionals.
  • Support: We strive to support our alumni throughout their careers with resources for continuous learning and professional development. By leveraging our diverse and talented community, we offer guidance and opportunities to help our members thrive in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
  • Give Back: It is our goal to cultivate a culture of giving back to the university and the broader community. Through scholarships, funding innovative projects, and volunteering, we contribute to the sustainability and success of the Chulalongkorn community and beyond.

Our Missions

  • To Promote Lifelong Learning: We are committed to promoting lifelong learning among our alumni by facilitating workshops, seminars, and guest lectures that reflect the latest industry trends and academic research in information systems.
  • To Encourage Alumni Involvement: We seek to actively involve alumni in shaping the future of our association and the university. Whether through participation in advisory boards, mentoring current students, or leading regional alumni chapters, our members play a critical role in our collective success.
  • To Enhance Professional Development: Our mission includes enhancing the professional development of our alumni by fostering opportunities for career advancement. This includes job placements, networking events, and professional workshops tailored to the unique needs of the MIS field.

By focusing on these goals and missions, the MIS Alumni Association of Chulalongkorn University aims to maintain a robust and supportive network that not only benefits our alumni but also contributes to the prestige and impact of our institution globally.
